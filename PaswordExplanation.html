As explained in the CodeMasterExplanation, you have 6 choices of colour and 4 slots to fill.
So, in the textbox, enter 4 characters. The characters allowed are:

  • "R" or "r" to indicate red. ()
  • "B" or "b" to indicate blue.()
  • "G" or "g" to indicate green.()
  • "Y" or "y" to indicate yellow.()
  • "C" or "c" to indicate cyan.()
  • "P" or "p" to indicate pink.()
